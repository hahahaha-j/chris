About Chris Cross

Chris Cross, the ingenious Discord/Guilded bot conceived by @thecatsmoo, transcends traditional server boundaries by introducing a revolutionary cross-channel functionality. Seamlessly intertwining conversations across different channels, guilds, and platforms, Chris Cross serves as a virtual bridge, fostering interconnected communities within the Discord/Guilded platforms. As users engage in dialogue in one channel, the bot ingeniously connects their discussions to corresponding channels in separate guilds. This innovative feature not only enhances communication but also facilitates the creation of expansive, unified communities. By breaking down the barriers between channels and guilds, Chris Cross redefines the Discord experience, promoting collaboration and camaraderie in a way that is both unique and transformative.
